Voice Note Therapy®

There is something therapeutic about releasing your thoughts and feelings via voice note. Whatever you may be dealing with or however you may be feeling, share it with your fellow Your Tribe members who can listen, reply and offer support.

Voice Note Therapy® should not be used in place of professional mental health support, counselling, psychiatric care or medical advice. Replies are not provided by registered therapists, and any advice shared should be approached with caution.

Voice Note Therapy® is not suitable for anyone experiencing suicidal thoughts, thoughts of self harm or a mental health crisis. If you need urgent support, please contact emergency services or a qualified mental health professional immediately.
